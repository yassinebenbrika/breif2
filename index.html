How Often Should I Rest?

Rest days are crucial for recovery and injury prevention. In general, aim for at least one to two rest days per week, but adjust based on your specific fitness goals and body's needs.

Can you exercise too much?

Excessive exercise can lead to overtraining, potentially causing physical and mental health problems. Maintain a balanced workout routine and pay attention to your body's signals for optimal results.

How often should I exercise?

For general fitness, aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity each week, along with two days of strength training.Adjust based on your specific goals and fitness level.
